TACTICAL WHALE RESPONSE TEAMS

The first line of defense against the threat posed by whales are the brave men and women that comprise SWOON's tactical response teams. Stationed in more than 25 countries across both hemispheres, they stand ready to respond to all reports of whale sightings in the sky, from the most pedestrian civilian report to the most dire whalefalls. 24 hours a day, seven days a week they stand ready to risk their lives in the defense of all those menaced by the constant threat of whales, willing to sacrifice so that you can rest assured that your safety is the number one priority of the Sky-Whale Overflow Observation Network.

COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Not only is SWOOP on the front lines in defending you from the largest mammals to ever breach the upper atmosphere, SWOOP has for decades been dedicated to the rebuilding efforts of those communities that find themselves blighted by an unusual concentration of whales. SWOOP contractors and specialists can be found in most afflicted communities and are happy to assist with rebuilding efforts and projects undertaken to reinforce homes and businesses against future incidents of whalefall.
